What is the process for a hot towel shave at your shop?
When you come in for a hot towel shave, we start with a skin consultation to assess your beard type and any sensitivities. We apply a pre-shave oil, then wrap your face in a steaming hot towel to soften your hair and open pores. After lathering up, our barber uses a straight razor for a close, careful shave. We finish with a cool towel, soothing aftershave, and moisturizer. The whole process is designed for relaxation and a smooth result.
