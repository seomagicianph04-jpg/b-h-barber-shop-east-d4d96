Frequently Asked Questions About Hair Styling & Blowouts

How much does a standard men's haircut cost at B & H Barber Shop in Newark, NY?
At B & H Barber Shop, a standard men's haircut is $20. If you’d like a wash and blowout included, the price is $28. We accept cash and major credit cards. Seniors and kids under 12 receive a $2 discount on haircuts.
How long does a typical haircut and blowout appointment take?
A standard haircut usually takes about 25–30 minutes. If you add a blowout or detailed styling, plan for about 40–45 minutes in total. We do our best to stay on schedule, but walk-ins may experience a short wait during busy times, especially on weekends.
Is B & H Barber Shop licensed and are your barbers experienced?
Yes, all our barbers are licensed by New York State and have several years of professional experience. We prioritize ongoing training to stay current with the latest styles and barbering techniques.
Can you walk me through your haircut and blowout process?
Absolutely! We start with a consultation to discuss your preferences and hair type. After a precision cut, we shampoo your hair (if you’ve requested a wash), then follow up with a blowout and light styling using professional products. We finish by checking the cut in the mirror and making any final adjustments you’d like.
What if I'm not satisfied with my haircut or style?
Your satisfaction is very important to us. If you're not happy with your haircut or blowout, please let us know before you leave the shop. We’ll be happy to make adjustments free of charge. If you notice an issue after you leave, just call us within 3 days and we’ll schedule a complimentary fix.
