Frequently Asked Questions About Beard Trimming & Shaping

How much does a beard trim or shaping cost at B & H Barber Shop in Jersey, NY?
Our beard trim and shaping services typically range from $18 to $28, depending on the length and style you're looking for. If you want a precise line-up or detailed shaping, it may be on the higher end. We’re always happy to give you a quote before we start, so feel free to ask your barber during your consultation.
How long does a beard trim and shaping appointment usually take?
A standard beard trim and shaping session usually takes about 20 to 30 minutes. If you’re looking for a more intricate style or if you’re pairing the service with a haircut, it might take a little longer. We encourage you to arrive a few minutes early, especially during busy times like weekends, since walk-in wait times can vary in Jersey City.
Are your barbers licensed and experienced with different beard styles?
Absolutely. All our barbers at B & H Barber Shop are fully licensed in New Jersey and have years of experience working with a wide range of beard types and styles—from tight fades and sharp lines to fuller, classic looks. We stay up-to-date with the latest techniques to make sure you leave looking your best.
What is the process for a beard trim and shaping at your shop?
We start with a quick consultation to understand your preferred length and style. Then, your barber will use professional clippers and scissors to shape your beard, followed by a straight razor for clean edges and defined lines. We finish with a warm towel and a beard conditioner or oil to keep your beard looking healthy. We always use sanitized tools and can adjust the process if you have sensitive skin or specific requests.
Do you guarantee satisfaction with beard trimming and shaping? What if I’m not happy with the result?
Your satisfaction is very important to us. If you’re not completely happy with your beard trim or shaping, please let us know before you leave the shop and we’ll make any adjustments you need free of charge. We want every client to walk out of B & H Barber Shop feeling confident with their look.
